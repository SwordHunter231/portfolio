About Me

I'm Elijah, a 17-year-old 3D animator and web designer currently balancing my creative work with high school. I discovered my passion for digital creation when I was 14 and have been honing my skills ever since.

What started as a hobby creating simple animations for friends has grown into a serious craft. I specialize in bringing products and brands to life through motion and interactive experiences.

When I'm not animating or coding, you can find me skateboarding, playing synth in a band, or binge-watching sci-fi movies for inspiration.
